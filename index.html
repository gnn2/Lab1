BATMAN


Batman is a comic book superhero who doesn't possess any superpowers.He is also known as "the Caped Crusader",
"the Dark Knight", and "the World's Greatest Detective". His real identity is Bruce Wayne and lives in the fictional city
of Gotham. He is a billionaire playboy, industrialist, and philanthropist. As a child he witnessed the murder of his parents
and swore revenge on criminals. He trained himself both physically and intellectually. He uses his intellect, detective
skills, cool gadgets, wealth, and martial arts skills to fight villains such as: the Joker, the Penguin, the Riddler, Two-Face,
Scarecrow, Poison Ivy and Catwomen. By his side are Robin- his partner in fighting crime and his butler Alfred Pennyworth.

He is featured in the Detective Comics. He was created by the artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger. He gained his
own comic book title in 1940. In the late 1960s the Batman television series were introduced. The Warner Brothers'
live-action films have been successful and have made the character even more popular in recent years. Actors such as
Adam West, Michael Keaton, Val Kilmer, George Clooney, Christian Bale, and Ben Affleck have all portrayed the
character in films. In 2012, the newest Batman film The Dark Knight Rises came out and received stellar reviews. Batman
was ranked second in Iamgine Games Network's Top 100 Comic Book Heroes of All Time Batman in 2011 and Empire
Magazine's 50 Greatest Comic Book Characters of All Time.
